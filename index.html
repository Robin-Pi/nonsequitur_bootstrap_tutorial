A Blog for Eccentrics

I've got something to say. It's better to burn out than to fade away. Most people will tell you their bad intentions. You just have to listen to hear and not listen, waiting to speak. They usually do it with a smirk, masquerading as a smile, and you don't explicity fall for it, you just close your mind to the obvious truth.

Find Your Own Tangent

I didn't hate the movie, "Inception", because hate is too strong a word to describe what was just a lack of interest for me. There is one take-away though that stuck with me. It was that of planting an idea into the subconscious. A germ. And, watching someone else running away with it.
This is what non sequitur is. A hodge-podge of ideas, of tangents and asides that may lead you to nowhere or somewhere you never thought you'd go.
